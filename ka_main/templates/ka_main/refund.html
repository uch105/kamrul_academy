At Kamrul Academy, we strive to provide high-quality educational experiences that empower learners. Refund Process: • To request a refund, please contact our support team at kamrulacademyofficial@gmail.com within the eligible timeframe. • In your request, please specify the course title and your reason for requesting a refund. • Once we receive your request, we will review it and respond within 2 business days. • If your request is approved, the refund will be processed using the original payment method within 2 business days. Please Note: • Processing times for refunds may vary depending on your financial institution. • We reserve the right to deny refund requests that violate our Terms and Conditions or if we suspect fraudulent activity. We encourage you to carefully review the course description and content before purchasing to ensure it meets your expectations.
